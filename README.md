## 📌 Problema
En México, el **92% de los microempresarios opera en efectivo**, lo que genera:  
- ⏳ Altos costos de tiempo.  
- 🔒 Riesgos de inseguridad.  
- 🚫 Falta de acceso a herramientas digitales modernas.  

---

## 💡 Solución
**QuetzaPay** es una **PWA (Progressive Web App)** que permite aceptar pagos digitales sin hardware adicional ni descargas.  

✨ **Características principales**:  
- **Offline-First**: registrar pagos sin internet y sincronizar después.  
- **Dashboards en tiempo real**: ventas, métodos de pago y actividad del comercio.

## 📌 Problem
In Mexico, **92% of micro-entrepreneurs operate primarily in cash**, which leads to:  
- ⏳ High time costs for operations.  
- 🔒 Increased security risks from handling cash.  
- 🚫 Limited access to modern digital tools and financial services.

---

## 💡 Solution
**QuetzaPay** is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** that enables merchants to accept digital payments without specialized hardware or app store installs.

✨ **Key capabilities**:
- **Offline-First**: record transactions while offline and sync when connectivity is available.  
- **Real-time dashboards**: view daily sales, payment methods, and merchant activity.  
- **Open-standards**: built on Interledger / Open Payments for interoperability and low transaction costs.
